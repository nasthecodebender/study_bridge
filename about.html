About Us

Dedicated to providing the best exam preparation resources in one place.

Our journey started inside the classrooms of Dandii Boru School in Addis Ababa, where students struggled to find organized, reliable national exam compilations. High-quality booklets were scattered across physical channels, and students lacked immediate feedback on their practice results.

StudyBridge was designed to digitalize this archive—bringing top-tier resources like Mr. Shishay's Mathematics Booklets and Grade 12 Biology genetics summaries, alongside fully timing-simulated exam suites, right into an aesthetic, responsive and easy web interface.
